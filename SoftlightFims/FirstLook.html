Concept of ‘Behind The Movement’

A research documentary movie with a duration of 130 minutes, undertook as a project by the students of Deen Dayal Upadhyay Gorakhpur University and Purdue University, USA in collaboration with the alumni and students of IIT Guwahati,  National Law Universities and other premier educational institutes of India (more details of the team members in crew section), unfolding the inside working methodology and the organizational setup focusing on the full time workers i.e. the Poornakaliks of the world's largest and one of the oldest students' organization- the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP).

The organization, ABVP, holding a legacy of 70 years, comprises of more than 3.2 million students as its members including professors, lecturers, teachers, academicians and intelligentsia from various premier educational institutes, traditional universities, colleges and higher secondary schools. Driven by the nationalistic ideology and mentored by its parent organization the Rashtriya Swayamsewak Sangh (RSS), the ABVP makes its presence across the length and breadth of the country through its 5500 active units on campus level, growing by each passing day.
